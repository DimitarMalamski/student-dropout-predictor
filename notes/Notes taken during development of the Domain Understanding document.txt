Sources of research, done for the Student Risk Identifier Model:

1. Student Drop-out from German Higher Education Institutions 

https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/ejed.12097

	- Dropout is a major higher education issue;
	- It affects students, institutions, and society;
	- Lower dropout rates are important for academic success and workforce supply;

	- Dropout is not the same as changing programme or pausing studies;
	- Clear definition is important because student mobility can be confused with dropout;
	- Measuring dropout is difficult when later student paths are unknown;

	- Dropout is usually multi-causal;
	- It develops over time rather than happening suddenly;
	- Different problems can accumulate until leaving studies seems unavoidable;

	- Main dropout-related factors:
		* performance-related difficulties;
		* high academic demands;
		* wrong study choice or wrong expectations;
		* financial difficulties;
